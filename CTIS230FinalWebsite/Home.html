Recipes Declassified: College Survival Guide

My Story

Hello! My name is Caden Alford. I am an incoming freshman at the University of North Carolina: Chapel Hill. After a stressful sophomore year filled with AP classes, I weighed 270lbs. This was the heaviest that I have ever been. I lacked energy, confidence, and many other things. About a year ago, we were launched into a global pandemic, forcing us to stay at home. I decided to use this time to my advantage and began changing my eating habits. I started with the Keto diet and many months later transitioned to a diet filled with clean and lean foods combined with a calorie deficit. Over the past year or so, I have been able to lose around 60lbs. I have now acquired much of what I lost being at my heaviest and much more. For example, I grew up being enveloped with a wide variety of foods and flavors. I’m a foodie. However, before my transition in eating habits, I never quite understood the power that food has on your body. At the end of the day, food is fuel for your body and mind. When you eat better, you feel better and I’m here to spread some of the recipes and tips that have helped me along my journey.

Mission Statement

Diets are often misconceptualized. People think that you have to eat tons of salad and bland food in order to lose weight and maintain healthy eating habits. The goal of this website is to provide recipes for food bursting with flavor. These recipes will make you feel like your eating habits have not changed one bit. Most people don’t have the time to spend in the kitchen cooking meals for hours. These recipes are quick and easy to make, especially for students with busy work schedules. The goal of this website is to empower students to take control of their diets and feed their minds. Welcome to Recipes Declassified: College Survival Guide! Let’s eat!
